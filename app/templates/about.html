ABOUT


Meet the developers

Roommate Finder is an app designed to make your life easier.

Forget about making public Facebook and social media posts to fill your apartment with roommates. Nor do you have to go through the logistics hassle of advertising your need for a space to live. Roommate Finder is designed to solve the problem of finding off-campus roommates by providing Rice students with a centralized and simple way to communicate to the entire Rice community their interest in finding new roommates. This application allows you to search for existing listings and create your own apartment listing.




The development team.

Roommate Finder is a project of Rice Apps, a student-led organization dedicated to making students' lives better through technology. We're completely open-source, and built on Python (Flask), SQLite, PHP, Bootstrap CSS, and a ton of fantastic JS libraries including jQuery, AngularJS, and PaceJS.
