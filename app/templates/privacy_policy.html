PRIVACY

POLICY


What information we collect and how we use it

Information we voluntary collect.

We only collect information that we explicitly ask for and that you explicitly give us. When signing in when your Net ID, authentication is controlled by the University; the only data we collect is your Net ID as a string, for authentication in this application. When you first sign in with your Rice Net ID, the application automatically looks up your Net ID in the Rice online directory and attempts to extract the following information: your full name, your residential college, and your class standing. When you create a profile, the data that you voluntary enter (including your profile image) is stored in a database on our server. If you choose to sign in via Facebook, the application receives (1) your Facebook ID, in order to associate you with a Facebook account, (2) your full name as it appears on Facebook, (3) your gender, and (4) your Facebook profile picture. These details will be stored only if you choose not to overwrite them when creating a profile. When you create a listing, the details you enter in the listing are also stored locally in a database on our server.


How your data is used.

The data you provide us is used to identify users who create and respond to apartment listings. It is also used to create {% if data.net_id and data.profile %}{% endif %}user profile pages{% if data.net_id and data.profile %}{% endif %}. Your data is never sold to advertisers and is never used outside of this application. However, the Roommate Finder application developers may browse through the databases for debugging purposes.


How your data is stored.

Your data is stored in a SQLite database (and as image files, if you choose to upload a profile picture) on a personally managed, dedicated server located in Los Angeles, CA. Data is automatically uploaded and stored in the databases as you use the application (i.e. creating or modifying an account, creating a listing, etc.). User profile images are hashed before being stored on the server; thus, there is no way to personally identify an image without the corresponding database entry. If you choose to {% if data.net_id and data.profile %}{% endif %}delete your Roommate Finder profile{% if data.net_id and data.profile %}{% endif %}, database entries associated with your Net ID will be automatically deleted. Note that your uploaded profile picture(s) will not be automatically deleted; please contact the server administrator (below) for manual deletion.


Further concerns.

For any further questions and concerns not addressed in this privacy policy, please directly email {% if data.net_id and data.profile %}{% endif %}Kevin{% if data.net_id and data.profile %}{% endif %}. This is also the point of contact for any concerns related to data storage, including manually purging data from the server.
