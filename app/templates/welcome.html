Here's how to get started

SETTING UP YOUR PROFILE.


You've already set up your profile! If you need to make any changes, including changing your profile picture{% if data.profile.facebook %} or disconnecting your Facebook account{% endif %}, go to My Account. From here, you can also view your current listings and delete your Roommate Finder account if you need to.

SEARCH FOR NEW ROOMMATES.


Want to see existing apartment listings and join someone looking for roommates? Use the main search interface by going to Find Roommates.

FILL YOUR EXISTING APARTMENT.


Do you already have an apartment and are looking for roommates to fill your apartment? Go to Create a New Listing to create a new apartment listing. From here, you'll be able to fill out details about the apartment and yourself, and your listing will be available to all other users of Roommate Finder.
